Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the solar system. It has a diameter of about 49,244 kilometers (30,598 miles) and is known for its striking blue color, which is due to the presence of methane in its atmosphere. Neptune has the strongest winds of any planet, reaching speeds of up to 2,100 kilometers per hour (1,300 miles per hour). It takes about 165 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun.
