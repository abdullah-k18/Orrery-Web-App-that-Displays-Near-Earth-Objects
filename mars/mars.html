Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. It has a diameter of about 6,779 kilometers (4,212 miles) and features the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons. Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, and it takes approximately 687 Earth days to complete one orbit around the Sun.
