Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's "sister planet" due to their similar size and composition. It has a diameter of about 12,104 kilometers (7,521 miles) and is the hottest planet in the Solar System, with surface temperatures reaching up to 465 degrees Celsius (869 degrees Fahrenheit). Venus has a thick atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid, making it a hostile environment. It takes about 225 Earth days to complete one orbit around the Sun.
