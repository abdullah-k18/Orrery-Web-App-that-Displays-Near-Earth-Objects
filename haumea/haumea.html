Haumea

Haumea is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt, known for its elongated shape and fast rotation. Discovered in 2004, Haumea has a diameter of about 1,632 kilometers (1,070 miles). It is unique among dwarf planets due to its two known moons, Hiʻiaka and Namaka, and it is also believed to have a surface covered in crystalline ice.
