Eris

Eris is a dwarf planet located in the scattered disc, a distant region of the Solar System. Discovered in 2005, it is one of the largest known dwarf planets and has a diameter of about 2,326 kilometers (1,445 miles). Eris takes approximately 557 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun. It has a moon named Dysnomia and is known for its highly elliptical orbit and significant distance from the Sun.
