Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is known for its unique blue-green color caused by the methane in its atmosphere. It has a diameter of about 50,724 kilometers (31,518 miles) and is the third-largest planet in the Solar System. Uranus is an ice giant and has a very low temperature, with the cloud tops reaching approximately -224 degrees Celsius (-371 degrees Fahrenheit). It orbits the Sun every 84 Earth years and has a distinct tilt of 98 degrees, which results in extreme seasonal variations.
