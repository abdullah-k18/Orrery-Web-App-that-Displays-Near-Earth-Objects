The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth largest moon in the solar system. It has a diameter of about 3,474 kilometers (2,159 miles) and is approximately 1/6th the size of Earth. The Moon influences Earth's tides and has a significant impact on its climate. Its surface is marked by craters, mountains, and flat plains called "maria." The Moon orbits Earth approximately every 27.3 days.
