Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its extensive ring system. It is the second-largest planet in the Solar System, with a diameter of about 120,536 kilometers (74,898 miles). Saturn is a gas giant composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, and it has numerous moons, including Titan, the second-largest moon in the Solar System. Saturn takes approximately 29.5 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun.
